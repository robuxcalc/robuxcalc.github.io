Understanding Robux to USD Conversion

Robux is the virtual currency used within the Roblox platform. Whether you're a player buying items or a developer earning through your creations, understanding how Robux converts to real USD is essential for making informed decisions.

Purchase Rate vs DevEx Rate

There are two primary conversion rates to consider. The purchase rate reflects what players pay when buying Robux ‚Äî approximately 80 Robux per dollar ($0.0125 per Robux). The DevEx rate is what developers receive when cashing out earned Robux through the Developer Exchange program ‚Äî currently $0.0038 per Robux for earnings after September 5, 2025.

How to Use This Calculator

Simply enter your Robux amount in the input field above and select your conversion type. For players wanting to know the value of potential purchases, use the Purchase Rate. For developers calculating potential earnings, use the DevEx Rate. The calculator instantly updates to show you the equivalent USD value.

DevEx Eligibility Requirements

To cash out through DevEx, developers must meet Roblox's eligibility requirements including having a verified account, being in good standing, and meeting minimum Robux thresholds. The minimum cash-out amount is 30,000 Robux.
