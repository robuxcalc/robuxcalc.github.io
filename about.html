Our Mission

We believe Roblox players and developers deserve transparent, easy-to-use tools for understanding the value of their virtual currency. Our calculator helps you make informed decisions about purchases and earnings.

Conversion Rates

We support two conversion types:

Purchase Rate ($0.0125/Robux): This reflects what players pay when buying Robux directly from Roblox. 80 Robux costs approximately $1 USD.

DevEx Rate ($0.0038/Robux): This is the rate at which developers can cash out earned Robux through the Developer Exchange program. Note that DevEx has eligibility requirements.
